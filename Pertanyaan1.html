Resep Makanan: Nasi Goreng

  • Bahan:

    • 2 cups cooked rice
    • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
    • 1 small onion, diced
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 cup mixed vegetables
    • 2 eggs, beaten
    • Salt and pepper to taste

  • Cara Membuat:

    • Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.
    • Add onion and garlic and stir-fry until they are translucent.
    • Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
    • Push the vegetables to one side of the wok.
    • Add a little more oil to the other side of the wok, then pour in the beaten eggs.
    • Scramble the eggs until they are cooked, then mix them with the vegetables.
    • Add cooked rice to the wok and stir-fry until everything is well combined.
    • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
